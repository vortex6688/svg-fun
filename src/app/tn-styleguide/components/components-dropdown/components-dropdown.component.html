Dropdown

The Bootstrap dropdown plugin allows you to toggle content on your pages

Dropdown Example

Dropdown Button Example

  • Item 1
  • Another Item
  • Some Other Item 
<div class="row">
  <div class="col">
    <div ngbDropdown class="d-inline-block">
      <button ngbDropdown class="btn btn-info" id="dropdownBasic" ngbDropdownToggle>Toggle Dropdown</button>
      <div class="dropdown-menu" aria-labelledby="dropdownBasic">
        <button class="dropdown-item" >Item 1</button>
        <button class="dropdown-item" >Another Item</button>
        <button class="dropdown-item" >Some other item</button>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
